# 🎯 Business Objective

Build an interactive analytics dashboard that enables users to explore the Data Jobs market by comparing salaries, hiring demand, employment trends, and job characteristics across multiple data professions. The dashboard supports career exploration and labor market analysis through interactive filtering and drill-through functionality.

---

# ❓ Business Questions Answered

This dashboard helps answer questions such as:

- Which data careers offer the highest salaries?
- Which roles are currently the most in demand?
- How has hiring demand changed throughout the year?
- How do yearly and hourly salaries compare across job titles?
- Which hiring platforms publish the most opportunities?
- What percentage of jobs are remote?
- Which positions require a college degree?
- How common are health insurance benefits?
- Which employment types dominate the market?

---

# 🚀 Dashboard Features

- Interactive filtering by Job Title
- Drill-through pages for detailed role analysis
- KPI cards for job count and salary metrics
- Hiring trend analysis over time
- Salary comparison across professions
- Scatter plot comparing hourly and yearly salaries
- Interactive salary heatmap
- Geographic distribution of job postings
- Employment type distribution
- Hiring platform analysis
- Work-from-home, degree requirement, and health insurance indicators

---

# 📈 Key Performance Indicators

| KPI | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Job Count** | Total number of job postings |
| **Median Yearly Salary** | Median annual compensation |
| **Median Hourly Salary** | Median hourly compensation |
| **Salary Rating** | Custom DAX measure summarizing salary competitiveness |
| **Hiring Trend** | Monthly job posting activity |

---

# 💡 Key Insights

- Data Engineer and Senior Data Scientist positions offer the highest median salaries.
- Data Engineer and Data Analyst roles represent the largest share of job postings.
- Hiring demand fluctuates throughout the year, showing seasonal recruiting patterns.
- Most opportunities are full-time positions.
- Remote work availability and benefits vary significantly across job titles.
- LinkedIn is the leading platform for publishing data-related job opportunities.
